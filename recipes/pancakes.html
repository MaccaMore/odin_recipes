Pancakes

Description

A recipe to make pancakes from scratch without using store bought mix
This recipes serves a total of 12 pancakes, and takes 25 minutes total to make.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups regular flour
  • 3 TBSP white sugar
  • 3 TSP baking powder
  • 3/4 TSP salt
  • 3 cup buttermilk
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/3 cup melted butter

Steps

  1. In a bowl add all dry ingredients together, these are:
    • Flour
    • Sugar
    • Baking Powder
    • Salt
  2. In seperate bowl combine all other ingredients including:
    • Milk
    • Buttermilk
    • Eggs
    • Melted Butter
  3. Beat together the wet ingredients with a whisk.
  4. Heat a frying pan over a stove at medium-high heat.
  5. Mix both bowls of ingredients together using a wooden spoon
  6. Pour 1/2 cup of mix onto the frying pan and cook on each side for 1-2 minutes
